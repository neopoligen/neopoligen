When the app is running, you can see a preview version of your site directly on your computer at this address:

localhost:1989

Once you've got it the way you want it, you can send it to a free service to make it available for world to see. See the documentation for details on how to do that.

Content Files

Neopoligen keeps all the files that make up your site directly on your computer. They're in a 'Neopoligen' folder under your main 'Documents' folder.
